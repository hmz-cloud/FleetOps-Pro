Everything a fleet needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Most fleet software is bloated and confusing. FleetOps does the four things that actually save you money — beautifully.

Cost & Budget Control

Track spend per cost center and per vehicle. See budget vs. actual at a glance. Catch overspending before it happens.

Driver Assignment

Know exactly who's responsible for every vehicle. Assign, reassign, and keep a full history. No more "whose car was this?"

Transfers & Approvals

Move vehicles between departments with a clean approval workflow and an immutable audit trail. Full accountability, zero spreadsheets.

Maintenance & Compliance

Never miss a service, insurance renewal, or inspection again. Automatic expiry alerts keep your fleet legal and on the road.

GPS Integration Ready

Already have GPS trackers? Connect Samsara, Wialon, Geotab, or any provider. See live positions with all your fleet data layered on top.

Analytics & Reports

Utilization, fuel breakdown, transfer trends, cost analysis — exportable to CSV. The numbers that drive better decisions.
